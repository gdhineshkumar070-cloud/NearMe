GYM

Crossing the threshold of a gym is an immediate immersion into an environment defined by physical exertion and mechanical energy. The air instantly feels different slightly warmer, more humid, and thick with a distinct, complex scent: a blend of sweat (both fresh and stale), the acrid tang of rubber flooring, the metallic smell of iron, and the sharp, chemical overlay of disinfectant spray. The auditory landscape is a chaotic, high-volume symphony. The foundational layer is usually bass-heavy, high-tempo music pumping from overhead speakers designed to keep energy levels high. Competing with the music is the relentless industrial hum of treadmills and ellipticals whirring in unison.
